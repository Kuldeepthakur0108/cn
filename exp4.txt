List scan: nmap -sL www.google.com

Ping Sweep: nmap -sP www.google.com

Disable ARP Ping: nmap -sn www.geeksforgeeks.com

TCP SYN Ping: nmap -PS www.geeksforgeeks.com

TCP ACK Ping: nmap -sA www.geeksforgeeks.com

UDP Ping: nmap -sU www.geeksforgeeks.com

IP Protocol Ping: nmap -v -PO www.geeksforgeeks.com

ARP Ping: nmap -PR www.geeksforgeeks.com

TCP Window Scan on Nmap: nmap -sW -T4 www.geeksforgeeks.com

nmap -sS -Pn 192.168.232.2 -O

Aggressive Scsn: nmap -A -Pn 192.168.3232.2




What is Nmap?

Nmap (Network Mapper) is a free and open-source tool used to scan networks and discover devices, services, and security weaknesses.

What Nmap does (simple points)

Discovers devices connected to a network.

Scans open ports on a system.

Identifies running services (like HTTP, FTP, SSH).

Detects operating systems of remote devices.

Helps find security vulnerabilities or weak points.
